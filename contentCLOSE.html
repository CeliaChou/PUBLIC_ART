合

「合」可喻為合作以及和諧，象徵著人與人之間的和諧往來，造型採用雙手交錯的形式，以磅礡宏大的氣勢中帶有簡潔又不失高雅的設計，放置於草坪上，讓空間感更為遼闊，並與複雜的環境、交錯的人流產生調和與對話。

作者:李光裕
創作年代:2001年
材質:銅
作品位置:台北市大安區市民大道四段178-188號/市民大道、延吉街口交叉口安全島

Close

“Close” was created in 2001 by Taiwanese artist, Lee, Kuang-Yu.
Two interlocking hands without any adornments is displaying on the Yanji street, aiming to bring the ideas of cooperation and harmony between people. Its majestic figure seems like building a relationship with the complex traffic around and have a conversation with the people.

Site:No. 178-188, Sec. 4, Civic Blvd., Da’an Dist., Taipei City
