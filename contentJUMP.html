飛躍東區

「飛躍東區」是現實與夢幻的結合，選用紅色來為這座城市增加力度與能量，讓色彩隨著時空變化來給予我們更多的動力，洗滌淨化我們的心靈，垂直的架構直指天際線也賦予著人間希望，並讓我們飛行於東區之際，記錄下每一份感動的瞬間。

作者:黃銘哲
創作年代:1999年
材質:不銹鋼
作品位置:台北市松山區民生東路三段156號/慶城街與敦化北路口槽化島

Jump

“Jump” was created in 1999 by Taiwanese artist, Huang,ming-zhe.
The artist chooses the color red to provide energy to the city and make a combination of reality and illusion. Also, the gesture which seems like it is running toward the sky is the idea of giving hopes to people, recording every steps that we moved and every efforts that we made. However, this thought-provoking public art may continuously keep passengers wondering what it is due to its special appearance.

Site:No. 156, Sec. 3, Minsheng E. Rd., Songshan Dist., Taipei City
