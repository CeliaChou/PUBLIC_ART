LOVE 台北之愛 台灣之愛

「愛」是這個世界上人們舉世共通的語彙，不論性別、種族、年齡的差異，「愛」佇立於人們每日穿梭往來的大樓前方，期許能夠拆除東西文化、種族、本土與國際間的藩籬，並向著所有人及全世界來發聲祈祝舉世的和平、共榮。

作者: 羅伯特．印第安那
創作年代: 2008年
材質: 鋁 、 漆料 、 鐵座
作品位置: 台北市信義區信義路五段7號（大樓前廣場）

LOVE

“LOVE” was a pop art image which was created in 2008 by American artist, Robert Indiana.
The artist believes love is the common word that people around the world will use in their lives. While this image of love is placed in front of the buildings that people will pass through everyday, the barriers between different nations and culture will probably be torn down one day and reach for a peaceful and prosperous world.

Site: No. 7, Sec. 5, Xinyi Rd., Xinyi Dist., Taipei City
