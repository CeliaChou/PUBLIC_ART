局chess

局chess的設計便宛如放大版的西洋棋，而行人們站立於棋局中等待著紅綠燈，也體現出人生如棋局般撲朔迷離，人們就像是那一顆顆直挺的棋子穿梭於世間的棋局之中。

作者: 溫子先
創作年代:2003年
材質:大理石
作品位置: 台北市信義區松壽路4號／市府路與松壽路轉角人行道鋪面

Chess

“Chess” was created in 2003 by Taiwanese architect, Andy Wen. For those passengers who are waiting traffic light at Shifu and Songshou road can take a look at this giant chess design. Andy Wen uses chess as a metaphor to symbolize our life because the same as the game of chess, no one knows what will happen next in the future, every single decisions we make will influence the result, but if we want to continue the game, all we can do is to make a move.

Site: No. 4, Songshou Rd., Xinyi Dist., Taipei City(intersection of Songshou Road and Shifu Road)
