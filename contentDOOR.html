科技之窗 人文之門

科技時代的到來，不僅加速了社會變遷更優先觸及了人們的思維與想法，因此將頭腦與CPU做結合，展現了現代群眾之思維與資訊科技之密不可分且息息相關。

作者:賴新龍 Hsin-Lung Lai
創作年代:2009年
材質:不銹鋼、烤漆
作品位置:台北市中正區市民大道三段10號

The Entry To The Technology And Humanity

“The Entry To The Technology And Humanity” was created in 2009 by Taiwanese artist, Lai, Hsin-Lung.
People nowadays have lived in the modern age of advanced technology, so the artist features the combination of our brain and CPU by expressing the inseparable relationship between our thoughts and the technology because our brain is the most important part of our body and technology is something that we can’t live without.

Site:No. 10, Sec. 3, Civic Blvd., Zhongzheng Dist., Taipei City
