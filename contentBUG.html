嗡嗡的風景

「嗡嗡的風景」以鐘罩造型創造出了「可聆聽的世界」，人們可以進到其中駐足休憩，並且聆聽自己內心的聲音，與現有都會型社會所營造的速度感產生對比，藉由東方藝術之獨有的靜默空間概念，打造出嶄新的視野。

作者:顧世勇
創作年代:1999年
材質:透明樹脂、鍍鋅防銹鐵架
作品位置:105台北市松山區敦化北路242號

Abuzz

“Abuzz” was created in 1999 by Taiwanese artist, Ku Shih-Yung.
Have you ever heard the voice of your heart? It may have some words to talk to you, but you may be too busy or afraid to listen to it. Now “abuzz” offers a chance to listen to your heart sincerely. You can just walk inside to relax, then you will realize that all you need is not a breathtaking view or a tasty meal, but a space of silence.

Site:No. 242, Dunhua N. Rd., Songshan Dist., Taipei City
