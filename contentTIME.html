時間斑馬線

紅燈、綠燈、紅燈、綠燈，就算你駐足，時間依舊不會為你停下，從利用日光竿影計時的日晷、緩緩流動的沙漏、滴答滴答的時鐘到路口不停切換閃爍的紅綠燈，皆闡明了時間的流逝，以車馬奔騰的街道作為起點，融入斑馬意象、光影、黑白、輕重韻律等馬路規則，讓人們游移在抽象及具體之間，並加上了創意及童趣，使其更加生活化。

作者:黃中宇
創作年代:1999年
材質:H型綱、強化玻璃
作品位置:台北市大安區敦化南路一段/敦化北路、市民大道口人行道上

Belisha Beacon

“Belisha Beacon” was created in 1999 by Taiwanese artist, Huang, Jhong-yu.
Time will never go back, just like if you miss the green light, you will have to wait for another one. While you are waiting for the traffic light of the intersection of Dunhua south road and Civic Boulevard, the traffic light on a zebra’s ass which named “Belisha Beacon” will probably attract your attention. By the importance of time, the artist takes streets as a start and combine the image of zebra and traffic rules in it in order to take our ease by admiring this creative and playful design.

Site:Sec. 1, Dunhua S. Rd., Da’an Dist., Taipei City
