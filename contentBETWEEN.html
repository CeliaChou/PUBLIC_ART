天地之間

「天地之間」是由八個半球體所組成的大圓，看似平凡的圓卻象徵著「天圓地方」、「八卦」的意涵，世間萬物無限輪迴，各種自然與人世間的現象不斷傳承下去，八顆球體宛如八卦的八種意念、元素相互搭配缺一不可，將世界的宏觀描繪於此地。

作者: 亞利安‧莫斯可維奇
創作年代: 2008年
材質: 粉紅色花崗岩 Rose de la claret granite
作品位置: 台北市信義區松智路277號（靠世貿二館之人行道上）

Between Earth and Sky

“Between Earth and Sky” was created in 2008 by French artist Ariel Moscovici.
Although the world is changing rapidly, our mind and belief will pass on, just like Between Earth and Sky consists of eight hemispheres that symbolize reincarnation and endless lives. The artist attempts to provide people a comfortable place to relax and have fun.

Site: No. 277, Songzhi Rd., Xinyi Dist., Taipei City(near the sidewalk of Taipei World Trade Center Exhibition Hall 2)
