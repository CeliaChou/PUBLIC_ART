稻草人

稻草人常被稱作農田的守護者，他們既不怕風也不畏雨，日復一日堅守著農地，以此作為出發點，作者以佈滿紋路的立方體，及疏密之間所隱藏之稻草人，帶出了頂天立地的精神和本土米食文化內涵。

作者:甘朝陽(甘丹)
創作年代:1999年
材質:主體材質花蓮雲紋白大理石、台座材質黑花崗石（噴砂面）
作品位置:台北市大安區敦化南路二段279號/敦化南路和平東路槽化島

Scarecrow

“Scarecrow” was created in 1999 by Taiwanese artist, Kan Tan.
The scarecrow always protects our farm without fear of wind and rain, as the soldier protects our country. Therefore, the artist comes up with the idea from scarecrow and creates this cube which covers with lots of lines. To observe it closely, you will probably see a scarecrow which was made of straw hiding between the lines. The image of scarecrow also brings out the idea of traditional rice culture and make a memorable impression to the passengers.

Site:No. 279, Sec. 2, Dunhua S. Rd., Da’an Dist., Taipei City
