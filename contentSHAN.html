山居

「山居」作品從傳統的學術思想出發，以大氣無裝飾的形式及雕塑手法，創造出簡單的美，「山」在傳統風水中與建築也有著密不可分的關係，而運用簡潔的線條及手法，更能體現東方的意象，並帶出其感性之氣息。

作者:黃清輝
創作年代:1999年
材質:黑花崗石
作品位置:台北市松山區敦化南路一段102-1號（八德路至市民大道間）

Mountain Ville

“mountain ville” was created in 1999 by Taiwanese artist, Huang Ching-Hui.
Mountain is one of the features in feng shui and architecture. By presenting the image of eastern culture, the artist creates this without any exaggerated decorations and only uses simple lines and drawing methods instead to let people know the beauty of ordinary.

Site:No. 102-1, Sec. 1, Dunhua S. Rd., Songshan Dist., Taipei City
