女武神·時間廊

「女武神·時間廊」以「迴旋穿透」及「時間差」作為設計的發想，世間萬物不斷循環更替，女武神便象徵著迴旋變遷終至周而復始的世代，並且以時間廊來做為時間差的代表，意欲將世間上所有美好的事物都停留在這個記憶的時間差之中，猶如記錄下人生中難忘的片段。

作者:董振平
創作年代:2001年
材質:花崗石 、 高碳鋼
作品位置:台北市松山區敦化南路一段126號/市民大道、敦化北路口西側中央分隔島

Deity，Time

“Deity，Time” was created in 2001 by Taiwanese artist, Dawn, Chen-Ping.
Do you forget things all the time? People always want to keep the wonderful memories in mind and leave the bad ones behind. Now a horse-riding deity which is located on the intersection of Dunhua north road and Civic boulevard can make your wish come true. “Deity，Time” refers to the idea of time lag and in hopes of collecting all the unforgettable moments in our life.

Site:No. 126, Sec. 1, Dunhua S. Rd., Songshan Dist., Taipei City
