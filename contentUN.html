韻—生命之旅

隨著地球的轉動，人們開啟了屬於自己的人生旅程，每一個瞬間、每一個階段都有屬於它自己的意義，作者運用圓形的外觀，來象徵著生命之旅的運行，期許人們在人生的脈動中，激起屬於自己的浪花，像車輪一般奮力向前邁進，實現自我價值。

作者: 何恆雄
創作年代:2001年
材質:銅
作品位置:台北市中山區市民大道一段258號

Rhyme

“Rhyme” was created in 2001 by Taiwanese artist, Ho, Heng Hsiung.
Due to the rotation of earth, people start their life journey. Every single step or moment has their own meaning, no matter the road is good or bad, why not just enjoy the ride? Therefore, the artist designed the round figure of “Rhyme” which symbolize our life journey, hoping people to roll like a tire and find their self-worth.

Site:No. 258, Sec. 1, Civic Blvd., Zhongshan Dist., Taipei City
