大力助手

「大力助手」的外型構造是取自於芬蘭雪人與日式盆栽的融合，也蘊涵中華文化風水的冀求。實木、天然石材的運用，宛如述說這大自然的生生不息，以樹枝作為雙臂，期許能給每一個路過的你一個大大的擁抱，雖然外層是了無生機的軀殼，但內部卻是富含強大的生命力，並給予著你我滿滿的正能量。

作者:馬可‧卡薩格蘭
創作年代:2016年
材質:非洲鐵木、天然石材、不銹鋼、青銅
作品位置:台北市大安區市民大道三段170號

The Great Assistant

“The Great Assistant” was created in 2016 by Finnish artist, Marco Casagrande.
Doesn’t it look familiar when you take a look at “The Great Assistant”? It isn’t a man nor a woman, but a combination of Finnish snowman and Japanese potting. The artist creates this sculpture with natural woods and stones in order to offer vitality and tell you the advantages of nature, so why not take a glance at “the great assistant”, it may wave you goodbye or give you a big hug with its wooden hands!

Site:No. 170, Sec. 3, Civic Blvd., Da’an Dist., Taipei City
