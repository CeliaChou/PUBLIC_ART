【城市藝站】活動紀錄

城市藝站在2019的冬季舉辦了兩場公共藝術體驗活動，分別是擺攤活動與入班教學，前者，我們將公共藝術融入彈跳乒乓球與記憶翻牌遊戲之中，讓大人小孩都能藉由參與活動的過程來認識公共藝術，後者，我們城市藝站親自到訪三峽介壽國小並安排一系列公共藝術教學與互動遊戲，藉以增加他們對公共藝術的興趣及認識。

[Public Art Station]Activities records

In the winter of 2019, public art station held two public art activities which were school celebration activity and teaching public art courses in the elementary school. During the school celebration, we connected public art with ball games and memory games so that adults and children could learn some information of public arts while they were participating the games. Moreover, we went to Jie-shou elementary school in Sanxia to teach them some knowledge of public arts and we also came up with some interesting in class activities for them, hoping to inspire their imagination and attract their interests in public art!
