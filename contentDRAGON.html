龍躍雲端

感官的饗宴、心動的感受，運用水柱的起伏與光影的幻化相互呼應，宛如龍之飛舞。「龍躍雲端」是以充滿強烈動態的雙龍抱珠概念來做基本架構，龍身設200個噴水頭，並以電腦控制水柱的高度及韻律；「珠」由25支大形噴水頭、78支變色燈光構成，透過電腦程式組合出萬象的變化，使整體畫面具有力度與美感，建構出絢麗的水舞秀。

作者: 水晶宮水景藝術設計公司 、 道格·達夫
創作年代: 2008年
材質: 綜合媒材Mixed media
作品位置: 台北市信義區信義路五段7號

Dragon feature-The finale show

“Dragon feature-The finale show” was created in 2008.
While walking through Shifu Road, you can hardly tell that Dragon Feature–The Finale Show is a piece of public art at first glance, but you will probably be amazed after seeing this gorgeous water show. It is consisted by 200 fountainheads and its height and rhythm of their output was controlled by computer. When the water is rising to the sky, the shadow and light lift just like a dragon, so it is a place that you can’t miss during night time and also a popular place for children and adults to enjoy in the summer too.

Site: No. 7, Sec. 5, Xinyi Rd., Xinyi Dist., Taipei City
