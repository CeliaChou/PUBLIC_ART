樹河（都市剪影）

樹、岩石、水皆為台北盆地最為原始的景物，然而曾幾何時，地球上最天然的資源已然被城市的文明建物所覆蓋，為了提醒我們這段記憶的存在，將樹木、石頭與水源再次結合，並運用生活化且趣味性的人物剪影來體現出現代都市的多樣性，到了夜裡，柔和的LCD燈光照射於其上，猶如來與去、實與虛、光與影的對比，夢幻般的燈光不僅拉近與人與人之間的距離，更為繁忙的都市生活添上一筆悠閒舒適。

作者:蔡淑瑩、陳健
創作年代:2000年
材質:花崗石、不鏽鋼鏡面、燈光、水
作品位置:台北市大安區忠孝東路四段135號

Tree River (Metropolitan Silhouette)

“Tree River” was created in 2000 by Taiwanese artist, Chen Chien and Tsai Shu-ying.
Trees, rocks and water were the original scenery of Taipei basin, however, these natural resources are now covered with the buildings. In order to remind us the existence of these resources, the artists combine these elements and human’s silhouette to underscore urban diversity. During nighttimes, the combination of illuminated shapes of trees and the real stream eliminate the gap between people and add a positive emotion to the city.

Site:No. 135, Sec. 4, Zhongxiao E. Rd., Da’an Dist., Taipei City
