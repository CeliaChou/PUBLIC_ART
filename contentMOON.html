星月交輝

90公分的花崗石球隨著水流緩緩轉動，水源沿著階梯由上而下顯現十足的動態效果，整體因蛋型構造的設計而使四方坡度有所不同，此具有動態感的水景公共藝術也富含了招財、好運源源不絕的意象。

作者: 水晶宮水景藝術設計公司 、 道格·達夫
創作年代:2008年
材質: 花崗岩片, Epoxy砂漿, 球行花崗岩
作品位置: 台北市信義區信義路五段7號（松廉路上人行道）

The rising sun-The shooting stars

“The rising sun-The shooting stars” was created by Doug Duff in 2008.
It is an egg-shape waterscape design with a 90cm- diameter granite ball rolling on the top. The water will flow down to different angles because of its egg-shape design, a wind speed sensor is included in order to turn down the jet power during windy times and the water jet will light up the night. This dynamic waterscape is not only providing us a cozy atmosphere but also a symbol of bringing wealth and luck.

Site: No. 7, Sec. 5, Xinyi Rd., Xinyi Dist., Taipei City(sidewalk of Songlian Road)
